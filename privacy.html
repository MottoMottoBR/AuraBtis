Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

Aurabtis ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Thunder Backup mobile application ("the App").

1. Data Collection and Usage

Thunder Backup is designed with privacy as a core principle. The App operates locally on your device to manage and secure your files. We do not collect, transmit, store, or mirror your personal files, photos, videos, or documents to our servers or any third-party cloud.

2. Required Permissions

To provide its core functionality, Thunder Backup requires certain permissions on your device:

  • Storage / Media Permissions: Required to access your files, photos, and videos exclusively for the purpose of backing them up or moving them into the encrypted Thunder Vault.

3. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect your personal files, the App integrates with selected third-party services to provide necessary functionalities such as premium subscriptions and analytics:

  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchases and verify premium subscription status securely.
  • Google Play Billing: Used to process transactions if you choose to upgrade to our premium features.
  • AdMob (Free Version): May be used to display advertisements to free users.

These services may collect anonymized device identifiers and usage data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures, including AES-256 encryption, to protect your data locally on your device. However, as no security system is impenetrable, we cannot guarantee absolute security against malicious hardware compromises or user negligence.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date".
